## 3) Smart itinerary (week by week)

### **Week 1: Santiago + Valparaíso**
- **Santiago (7–10 days)**
  - Neighborhoods: **Providencia / Lastarria / Barrio Italia**
  - Highlights: Cerro San Cristóbal, Santa Lucía, Museo de la Memoria
  - Food: Mercado Central, La Vega, empanadas, pisco
  - Day trips: **wine valleys** (Maipo / Casablanca)
- **Valparaíso + Viña (2–3 days)**: street art, funiculars, coast

### **Week 2: Araucanía (Pucón base)**
- **PN Conguillío** (volcanic landscapes, araucaria forests)
- **PN Huerquehue** (high‑altitude lakes)
- Optional: **Villarrica Volcano** ascent (guided)

### **Week 3: Lakes District**
- **Puerto Varas / Llanquihue**
- **PN Vicente Pérez Rosales**
- Volcano viewpoints (Osorno / Calbuco)

### **Week 4: Chiloé**
- **Castro** base + **Tantauco Park**
- Island culture + coastal hikes

### **Weeks 5–7: Carretera Austral (north → south)**
- **Pumalín** (temperate rainforest, fjords)
- **Queulat** (hanging glacier)
- **Cerro Castillo** (alpine trek)
- **PN Patagonia (Cochrane)**

### **Weeks 8–9: Patagonia**
- **Puerto Natales** base
- **Torres del Paine** (W Trek 4–6 days)
- Optional: **El Chaltén** (Argentina)

### **Week 10–12: Buffer + return to Santiago**
- Flex days for weather
- Return to Santiago for final days and departure

## 5) Internal transport (simple & realistic)
- **Overnight bus**: Santiago ↔ Temuco/Pucón
- **Regional buses**: Pucón → Osorno/Puerto Montt
- **Bus + ferry**: Puerto Montt → Hornopirén → Pumalín
- **Carretera Austral**: segmented buses (book early in high season)
- **Patagonia**: Puerto Natales ↔ Torres del Paine (daily buses)
